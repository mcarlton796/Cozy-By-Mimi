Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I place an order?

    Please navigate to the google form link found in my bio here or in the collections page here to place an order! Once I receive your details, I'll confirm your order by email and send payment instructions.

  • What payment options do you offer?

    Currently i accept payment through zelle and paypal! I'll open up more options as my business grows.

  • When is payment expected?

    For collection orders full payment is expected before the order can be fulfilled. For commissions - please refer to the commissions page for more detailed information here.

  • How can I purchase from a different collection?

    Collections other than the first drop are announced a week ahead on my social media pages and are open for a limited time. Previous collections may or may not return. If an old collection is available for purchase, the corresponding form will be available.

  • Where do you ship to?

    I am located in California, USA - I ship using USPS, so wherever they ship to I am happy to ship to as well. For more detailed information please refer to the shipping page here.

  • Do you accept returns?

    Due to the personalized nature of the items, at this moment returns are not available. However a refund may be issued at a case by case basis if necessary - determined by a thorough investigation.

About

Cozy by Mimi was born out of the love of creating and using it as a way for me to slow down in the midst of such a chaotic and busy life. I want to spread joy with my little trinkets and I hope that you love each piece as much as I loved creating them. Thank you so much for stopping by!

Clay Items

  • Smaller items like charms and hippers are made with polymer clay, while items like trinket dishes are made with air dry clay.
  • Paints are gouche and acrylic
  • Glazes are of UV resin
  • Some pieces will have magnets, armature out of wire, etc.

Chrochet Items

  • Chrochet items are amde out of acrylic yarn, specifically chenile for plushies.
  • Plushies contain polyfill for their filling.
  • Items contain plastic safety eyes but are not recommended for younf children.
